Process

Up and running in three steps

01

Feed your knowledge

Paste your FAQs, product descriptions, pricing, or any business information. Buvo learns your business in seconds.

02

Customize the widget

Match your brand colors, set a persona name, define the tone of voice. The widget becomes yours.

03

Paste one script tag

Copy a single line of HTML into your website. The AI assistant goes live instantly — no backend, no devs needed.

Capabilities

Everything you need to delight your customers

Buvo is more than a chatbot — it's a full AI customer success layer for your website.

Instant answers, 24/7

No wait times, no ticket queues. Customers get accurate answers the moment they ask — at 3am or 3pm.

Your knowledge, your rules

Train on your exact content — support docs, product pages, pricing tables. The AI never guesses outside your data.

Human handoff

When a question is too complex, Buvo seamlessly escalates to your team — with full conversation context attached.

Works on any website

WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, plain HTML — if it has a <body> tag, Buvo works on it.

Analytics dashboard

See what customers ask most, where conversations drop, and how the AI performs — all in one clean dashboard.

Multiple languages

Buvo detects the customer's language and replies in kind — supporting 40+ languages out of the box.

Integration

One line of code.
That's it.

Paste the snippet before the closing </body> tag. Your AI assistant is live within seconds. No build step, no npm packages, no backend setup required.

index.html 
<!-- Paste before </body> -->
<script
  src="https://cdn.buvo.io/widget.js"
  data-key="your-api-key"
  data-color="#c8f060"
  defer
></script>
